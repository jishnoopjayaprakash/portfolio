About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering with over 8 years of experience in academia and industry. My research focuses on thermal management of electronic systems, renewable energy integration, and advanced manufacturing processes. I hold a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Madras and have published 25+ peer-reviewed papers in high-impact journals.

Previously, I worked as a Senior Research Engineer at Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) and served as a Postdoctoral Fellow at NUS Singapore. I am a member of ASME, IEEE, and the Indian Society for Technical Education.
